1997 TRASH Regionals: the Andy Sidaris Spectacular

The University of Maryland, College Park Academic Quiz Team announces that it will be hosting a TRASH regionals tournament: the Andy Sidaris Spectacular, on Sunday, November 2.

The Andy Sidaris Spectacular will feature questions written by TRASH, which implies that there are no question submission requirements for participating teams, and that the questions used will be of the highest quality.

The price guide is as follows:

Base price $70 per team

-$30 off the total price for teams attending the Terrapin Invitational
Tournament and Andy Sidaris Spectacular double-feature.
-$10 for a working buzzer system (discount can be applied for both the
Terrapin and the Sidaris)
-$10 for second, third, etc. teams from a school. "Bastard" and Masters
teams are not considered to belong to a school and are thus ineligible for
this discount.
Minimum price for any team is $20.

Checks may be made payable to the Maryland Academic Quiz Team and sent to the address listed below:

    Shaun Hayeslip
    2114 Anne Arundel Hall
    University of Maryland
    College Park, MD 20742
Rules/Features of the Andy Sidaris Spectacular:

  • Teams representing specific schools must be composed of undergraduate (part-time or full-time) or graduate students attending the school. Masters teams are also welcome and are eligible for playoffs.
  • Games will be untimed, and there will be no timeouts. There also will not be power tossups. For more information on TRASH tournament play, contact Fred Bush at fred@condor.sccs.swarthmore.edu or your local TRASH representative.
  • Trophies for the top two teams and prizes for the top five individual scorers.
  • A minimum of twelve rounds will be read.
Please direct inquiries regarding rules or registration to David Hamilton at dhamiltn@wam.umd.edu.
