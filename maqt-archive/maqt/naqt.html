NAQT Mid-Atlantic Sectional

The University of Maryland-College Park is pleased to host the Mid-Atlantic Sectional for National Academic Quiz Tournaments. The tournament will take place on Friday evening and Saturday, November 15-16, 1996.

NAQT was created by a group of tournament veterans with the aim of providing quality questions at a low cost. The format, question difficulty, and question distribution will all follow the guidelines of the Penn Bowl/Michigan MLK Tournaments. Each game will consist of two 9-minute halves. Questions will be drawn from mostly academic subjects (history, literature, science, etc.), though non-academic subjects such as current events, pop culture, and sports will also be represented. For more information about NAQT see the NAQT web site or send email to naqt@naqt.com.

To register for NAQT Regionals at Maryland, contact Lord High Tournament Director Jesse Molesworth via email at: potemkin@wam.umd.edu or via telephone at (301) 314-0207. The last day to register for this tournament is November 9.

The tournament fees will be the following:

$70 base fee
-$10 for bringing a working buzzer system
-$10 for bringing a working clock
-$20 for teams who have competed in two or fewer tournaments within the
past year (ACU-I events excluded)

Question sets will be available after the tournament for the price of $25.

A preliminary cap of 30 teams has been set. However, if there is sufficient interest, we will expand the tournament as resources permit. Priority will be given initially to first teams from each college or university. The exact tournament format will be determined by the size of the field; however, each team can expect to play at least 15 round-robin matches. If brackets are necessary, a round-robin playoff (similar to ACF Nationals) will be used.

Jesse Molesworth
Tournament Director, NAQT
